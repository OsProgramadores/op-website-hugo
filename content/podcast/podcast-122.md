In this episode, Marcelo talks with Daniel Stenberg, the founder and CEO of curl  — the open-source command-line tool and library that powers data transfers across the internet. Curl runs on billions of devices, from your computer to your car, and underpins much of the modern web.

Daniel shares his experience maintaining one of the world’s most critical open-source projects for over 25 years. He talks about the challenges of scaling open-source software, and how passion, community, and discipline fuel long-term innovation.
